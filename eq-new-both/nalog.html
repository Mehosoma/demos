Tax planning

Taxation in conducting deals and transactions with Swiss companies is one of the most important factors of business. The task of the Tax Office is not only timely and correct preparation of tax reports, but also competent planning of the tax burden, realistic assessment of tax risks and management of the effective group tax rate, regardless of how wide the geography of the business is.

Equilibris Management AG provides assistance in managing tax risks and eliminating unjustified tax losses occurring when working with Swiss partners.

Equilibris Management AG offers:
  • Consulting clients on tax planning, including:
    • Analysis of tax opportunities for businesses to enter new markets;
    • Elaboration of tax transactions aspects for the acquisition of assets abroad;
    • Selection of the most suitable foreign jurisdiction;
    • Consulting and advising on the specifics of legislation and taxation of individual subjects in the Swiss confederation (various cantons have various tax rates).
  • Registration of various company types in the Swiss confederation.
  • Asset protection (trusts and foundations)
  • Legal support and maintenance, including:
    • Timely preparation and submission of financial statements;
    • Payment of state duties and fees;
    • Submission of the necessary legal documents and reports to state authorities, obtaining the necessary permits, as well as compliance with the entire document flow in accordance with the valid legislation in the specified territory related to this company;
    • Opening bank accounts in large Swiss banks: UBS AG, Credit Suisse AG, Raiffeisen-Group, etc.

    The service cost is established individually for each client based on the combination of the following factors:

  • Specifics of the economics and organizational structural of the client;
  • Client's needs in diagnosing the contractual system of relations and the volume of document workflow within the legal structure;
  • Specialist’s labor cost.
