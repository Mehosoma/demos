Trade finance

We offer our partners services in organizing the financing of foreign trade contracts using various instruments (including letters of credit and guarantees), including non-standard and complexly structured schemes, in particular, using letters of credit as trade finance instruments.

It is based on the attraction of guarantees from European banks for a period of up to 10 years (including under ECA insurance coverage). More than 70% of exporters worldwide are already using this tool.

Equilibris Management AG consolidates large assets as a collateral and attracts financing for them from a foreign bank. Bank does not use real money, but operates it only in case of borrower’s default. Thus, your business gets an additional grace period of 90 to 210 days.

Lending at the expense of foreign banks allows to obtain short and mid-term financing on more profitable terms.

The advantages of this type of lending:
  • Possibility to choose the most suitable financing term;
  • Pricing terms are more attractive compared to direct lending;
  • Support on all stages of the transaction by highly qualified specialists of the bank;
  • Flexible pricing;
  • Ability not to divert your own funds at the time of payment on the submitted documents under the letter of credit.

Due to pool increase of attracted assets, we expand the list of clients and invite companies to place a new financing package.
