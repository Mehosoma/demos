Questions about your situation? We’re here to help.

Our firm is designed to help with estate and trust situations following the death of a loved one. You may feel lost, disoriented, or overwhelmed with everything that is going on. You may feel an urgency to start doing something, and you want to make sure it is done right. Or, there may be someone abusing the estate, and you know you need to stop it.

Our legal team understands your situation. We help our clients with these issues every day. In fact, that is all we do. We know you will have questions – we want to educate you on how the estate settling process works. We start that with a private, confidential, complimentary consultation

How does it work?

You will speak one-on-one with a dedicated member of our probate team. We will take the time to listen to you and ask questions to learn more about your situation, what your goals are, and what is important to you.

Then, we will help you understand the legal backdrop and some of the legal issues at play. Next, we will discuss the options that can start to move your situation towards a solution. Since every situation is different, there are usually several options to discuss.

After we select the best option for your situation, if you decide you want a solid legal team behind you, our attorneys will leap into action to start moving things in the right direction.

Either way, you leave the consultation with clarity about your situation and a better understanding of the legal backdrop
