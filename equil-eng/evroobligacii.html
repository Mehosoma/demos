EUROBONDS

An alternative to bank deposits for best preservation of capital. Equilibris AG’s conservative strategy of trust management based on high capital protection level and coupon profit generation through investing into the bonds of Russian and foreign publicly owned companies.

Strategy

Portfolio diversification by countries, sectors and issuers, as well as the change in portfolio duration and average level of credit risk. The coupons will be reinvested as they are received.

Who it is for

It is for customers whose main goal is to find a viable alternative to ruble deposits and provide best capital protection.
