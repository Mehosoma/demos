We are Kally Sleep

We are the sleep experts. Not just because we love our sleep, but because we know how much sleep can impact daily life.

We believe everyone deserves a good night’s sleep and that starts with solving any unwanted sleep problems.

After an extensive study into sleep by talking to Harley Street doctors, Osteopaths, Chiropractors, Physiotherapists, Sleep Apnea consultants and just about everyone we knew, we were able to design unique products to tackle some of the most common sleep issues. Whether its snoring, neck, back and hip pain, anxiety, a sports-related injury or sleeping with a baby bump (the list goes on!).

Kally Sleep launched its first range of problem-solving products in December 2018 starting with the Kally Body Pillow, which provides lightweight, body aligning, side sleep. The Body Pillow has helped over 40,000 people to get a better night’s sleep, and over the last two years we have continued to create sleep-solving pillows for each type of sleeper. Whether you’re a side, back or front sleeper we can find you the perfect pillow and fix your sleeping problems. We even have a pillow quiz that can help.
