About Civil PE Practice

Civil PE Practice is a team of licensed civil engineers with the common
goal of helping you pass the Civil PE Exam.

We felt there was a shortage of high quality, online practice problems. As a result we developed this website. Our goal is to weed out the excess information and present the useful information in a clear, concise manner. We strive to produce the most comprehensive and representative PE practice problems available. Each of our problems meet the latest NCEES standards and cover topics found on the NCEES specification page.

We love feedback and are eager to improve our site. Please reach out to us with your questions and feedback so that we can make this the best PE Exam site available. Good luck on your upcoming PE Exam.
