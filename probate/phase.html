Phase 1: Getting appointed

The goal of Phase 1 is to have someone appointed as the Administrator of the estate by the Probate Court. To accomplish that, you will prepare a Petition for Letters of Administration, prepare other necessary documents depending on your situation, and file all of the documents with the Probate Court. At the end of Phase 1, you will receive Letters of Administration from the Probate Court, which is the Court Order that officially makes you Administrator of the Estate. When you receive the Letters of Administration, it will signify that you are ready to begin Phase 2.
