Phase 1: Getting appointed

Determine the Heirs

Use this checklist to determine the heirs of the estate. Heirs must be determined regardless of whether or not the deceased left a Will.

If no Will was left, then the heirs will inherit property from the estate. Georgia Law requires all heirs to be identified on the Petition for Letters of Administration.

If a Will was left, the heirs will not inherit from the estate (unless the heir is also identified as a beneficiary in the will). But, Georgia Law requires that the heirs be properly identified on the Petition to Probate Will in Solemn Form. Each heir must either consent to the Petition (by signing an Acknowledgement of Service and Assent to Probate Instanter), or must receive formal notice of the petition from the Probate Court.
